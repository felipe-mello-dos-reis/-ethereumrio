The Blockchain Technology

The blockchain is an immutable distributed digital ledger, which means you can always trust it to be accurate and is safe from network attacks.

Patient Area

Log in here if you are a patient. In this area, you will be able to search for doctors in your area, each one with their respective expertise. You will also be able to check each doctor's history and the recommendations they got from previous patients.

Doctor Area

Log in here if you are a doctor. You can check the patients that would like to consult you. Start attending them by uploading your credentials in this area.

Comitee Area

Comitee area allows members to get to know the patients who need help and decide whether or not to authorize the donated money to be used to cover the costs of their treatment.
